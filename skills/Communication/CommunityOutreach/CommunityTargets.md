Jm's target communities for outreach, derived from TELOS goals. Each community has specific discovery sources, criteria, and priority levels.

## High Priority Communities

### Professional AI / Tech (San Diego)
**Goals:** G12 (Find Professional Community), G26 (Find a Cofounder)
**Why:** Highest ROI -- serves both community-building and cofounder search simultaneously.

**Discovery Sources:**
- San Diego AI/ML Meetup groups
- Local tech conferences and hackathons
- LinkedIn (San Diego + AI/ML/security)
- Hacker News users with SD location
- Y Combinator startup community (SD-based founders)
- AI/tech podcasters based in SD
- Open source contributors in AI/ML space

**Ideal Contact Profile:**
- Building or working in AI/ML/security
- Based in San Diego or Southern California
- Entrepreneurial mindset (cofounder potential)
- Active in community (speaks, writes, organizes)
- Technical depth with product sensibility

**Tags:** `professional-ai`, `cofounder-potential`, `local-sd`
